THE UNIT

Measure with your hand.

DETAILS

The Unit

Round 1:QUIZEC

This round will contain general technical quiz.

Round 2: PICTURE PERCEPTION

The participants will be shown 30 different images (tech). The participants need to identify, write the name and use of the object.

Round 3: Dimensional Danger

In this round, each team will be given a sample part to check for dimensions. They will be given different instruments like Standard calipers, Vernier Calipers, Micrometer Screw gauge, Vernier Micrometer, Vernier height gauge, etc. (No dial or digital instruments are to be provided). Participants will have to determine various quantities like outer diameter, inner diameter, thickness, mean diameter, etc. The closest and most accurate answers are to be considered.
