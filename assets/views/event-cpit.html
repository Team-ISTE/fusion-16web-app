Big Bang Theory

Round 1:Buzz-Up

This is a buzzer round event in which they have to identify IT/CP based companies,persons,etc. There will be questions which include logo, tag line, picture to identify (ceo, place, etc).Number of participants will be divided into small groups and they have to answer as quickly as possible by pressing buzzer. All the questions are shown on projector to participants.

Round 2:Speak the Pic

All the participants will be paired up according to their performance in 1st round. They will be shown two pictures (one technical and the other one non-technical). They have to relate both the pictures and speak on it for 3 minutes. They also have to give their introduction in it. Here they will be judged on their speaking skills.

Round 3:Big Bang

In this round selected participants will be given a question(long story) and they have to make a program based on that questions(situations). Coding will be done on C++.
