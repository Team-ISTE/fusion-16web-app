DETAILS

The Maze Runner

Round 1: - Preliminary quiz 

The first round of the event is based on general quiz based on Basic Of electronics. Like analog, digital etc.


Round 2: - Arduino Coding

A problem statement would be asked on basic Arduino programming (like LED blink, display, serial communication etc.). They have to display the working Arduino code in Arduino IDE.

 

Round 3: - Arduino Implementation

In final round problem statement would be given and they have to write code and implement circuit on Proteus tool. Round 3 is almost on the base of practical application like LCD display, motor controlling, led controlling etc. So, the problem statement will be given on the spot and the participants will have to solve the problem(s) within the specified time limit.

White Collar

A technical event, examining your creativity, presence of mind and teamwork platform from basic knowledge of your stream.

DETAILS

White Collar

Round 1:Know’em all

This round comprises of various pictorial images. For eg. A picture of bulb will be shown. Then below it will be the question related to the inventor of it.For eg: This man brought ‘LIGHT’ in our lives. Who is he. Vice-versa will also be done. Eg. A picture of some famous personality, say Steve Jobs will be shown. “What made him famous in the world?”


Round 2: Jumble Trouble

In this round a circuit will be provided to each of the qualified teams but only one of the two team members can see the circuit. Then that member will have to explain the circuit to the other team member, without using the exact terminologies.

 

Round 3:Relay

In this round the qualified teams have to select a member,the selected member will be given a starting clue, then he has to solve it and gain the clue for the required components. Similarly he will have to obtain 2 components, then return to his partner and forward the clue for the 3rd component and then he has to carry forward the relay and has to find 2 other components and finally the team that gets all the components will be given a master component by us and they have to make the circuit on paper and the team completing in minimum time will be the winner.

Participants per team – 2
